Self Development:

How to find more Purpose & Clarity:

1. Rate yourself throughout the day to see how well your doing

2. Observe things you like & don't like during the day. Take note & try to do more of that the next day.

3. Get a mentor/coach for what your trying to achieve. There is someone else out there doing the same thing your trying to do, & have done it at an exceptional standard. Read biographies or join their online courses.

4. Observe things your doing our of obligation, expectations or reactions. You want to move beyond this.

6. Slow down & enjoy what your doing in that moment. If it's playing with your kids do that fully, if it's doing your art do that fully, if it's talking on the phone to your family do that fully.

Use annoyance as a cue to stop & breath.

Three reasons you might be feeling horrible:

1. Fatigue

Haven't slept well, haven't eaten enough of the right foods. Haven't been doing enough exercise.

2. Triggered, annoyed, unsettled or upset at something that happened earlier in the day. Someone cut you off in traffic, had a bad interaction with someone.

3. Rumination

Replaying events & emotions over & over in our head. Playing out arguments in out head.

Ways to stop feeling horrible:

1. Get up, breath, stretch, have a glass of water & choose your next act of integrity. This might be something as simple as cleaning up, or putting a load of washing on. What ever makes you feel like your taking back control & moving forward in your life.

2. Get up & move, go for a walk or do some pushups.

3. Change the enviroment. Get out of the house, go for a walk or drive.

4. Read something inspirational like a self improvement book or quote, or watch something inspirational on youtube.
