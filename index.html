Welcome to my blog!

This is my blog where I document all the cool exciting stuff I'm doing! (at least it's exciting for me 😝). My favourite things to do are code in Dart & Flutter making cool apps & games, self studying physics and hanging out with my family playing video games or playing in the outdoors. 

Here I'm going to document my progress on all these things. My goals are to find paths that work for me and hopefully distill this down into a process that I can teach to other people. 

I've always wanted to make games and have learnt a lot over the years, but it's always a process of distilling and refining your process, what you want to experience out of it and what value you can give to others. My journey at the moment is to learn more about how to build semi-casual games in Flutter that I can release to the App & Play store that feel like real games. I don't like wasting people's time or giving them a bad experience. I want to add to their lives, so I'm following that route and I'm not into adding addictive things.
